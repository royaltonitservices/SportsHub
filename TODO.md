# SportsHub — What's Done, What's Not

**Last Updated:** April 4, 2026

---

## Done

- Sign up and log in (email/password, keeps you logged in)
- Switch between Basketball, Football, Soccer, Tennis
- Find opponents and challenge them to matches
- Accept challenges and submit match results
- Trust scores and warnings for sketchy players
- Dispute a match result and upload evidence
- View dispute history
- Leaderboards per sport
- Tennis court finder (real courts near you)
- Send and accept friend requests
- Block and unblock people
- DM your friends (1-on-1 chat)
- Post to the feed (create, like, comment, delete)
- Comment threads on posts
- Quick reactions on posts
- Upload video clips
- AI Coach chat (text and voice)
- AI Coach floating widget on every screen
- AI Coach generates drills and challenges
- Daily readiness score (how ready is your body)
- Weekly personalized drills (premium)
- Goals survey (pick what you want to improve)
- Smartwatch sync (Apple Watch, HealthKit data)
- Premium subscription (monthly/yearly, real Apple Pay)
- Premium upgrade prompts for free users
- Tournaments (browse, create, join, brackets, standings)
- Onboarding tutorial for new users
- Profile with stats per sport
- Edit display name and username
- Admin dashboard (stats, user management, content moderation)
- Report inappropriate content
- Dark mode
- Settings page (profile editing, preferences, logout)
- Skill progression tracking (local, radar chart)
- Drill library (hundreds of drills per sport)
